Nicks book of AFL rules

This book is a detailed look at all the rules used in AFL football. It provides details on what happens when a player is accidentally knocked out with a closed fist.

Nicks book of soccer rules

by Karen Smith

This book explaoins what happens when a soccer player finger nail breaks during the game and he starts to cry.

Nick golf rules

by Lewis Cinni

This book explains how to best get a hole in one whenever you are playing on your own and no one else sees it.

Nicks book of rock and roll chords

by Eddie Van Halen

This book explains how to play all the chords that you ever need for every rock and roll song. Then it demonstrates how to play all 3 of them.
